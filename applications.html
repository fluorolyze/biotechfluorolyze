Steps to treatment without Fluorolyze

1. A patient is showing signs of a stroke and calls an ambulance

2. Average of 8-15 minutes for ambulance to arrive

3. 30 minutes for the patient to reach hospital

4. 45 minutes to reaches neurology department

5. 135 minutes is needed before the stroke team is called

6. 90 minutes to initiate a CT/MRI scan

7. 300 more minutes till stroke diagnosed and treatment is given

Patients with a stroke can wait up to 10 hours before receiving treatment (Harpaz).

Steps to treatment with Flourolyze

1. A patient is showing signs of a stroke and calls an ambulance

2. Average of 8-15 minutes for ambulance to arrive

3. Fluorolyze used to determine probability of stroke occuring

4. 30 minutes for stroke team to be called

5. 45 minutes to initiate a CT/MRI scan

6. 90 more minutes till stroke diagnosed and treatment is given

Using flourolyze, up to seven hours can be cut and patients can receive the care they need much faster.
